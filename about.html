Good Food. Cold Drinks. Real Fun.

Welcome to The Bear Trap—Sauk Centre’s favorite place to unwind.

Simple. Delicious. Welcoming. We focus on craveable food, the best drink deals in town, and an easygoing atmosphere where locals and travelers alike feel at home. We’re also your hub for entertainment — we play all the big games and major sporting events live on our screens, and we offer pull tabs and iPad slots so you can play right from your seat. Pull up a chair — we’ve got a seat waiting for you.

Bold Food. Cold Drinks.

We’ve raised the bar on pub dining — fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and drinks that keep the good times flowing.

  • Fresh, Never-Frozen Burgers: 100% fresh beef, seared hot and stacked high.
  • Legendary Pizza: Crispy crust, rich sauce, and generous toppings.
  • Fresh Salad Bar: Available on select nights for something crisp and light.
  • Unbeatable Happy Hours: Big value on drafts, wells, and rotating specials.
  • Your Drink, Your Way: Domestic taps, craft pours, and handcrafted cocktails.

The Bear Trap Promise

Why do people choose us? It’s simple.

  • Uncompromising Flavor: If it’s not delicious, it doesn't leave the kitchen.
  • High Energy, Low Stress: A welcoming vibe where you can relax, laugh, and win.
  • Honest Pricing: Premium quality food and entertainment without the premium markup.
