About The Bear Trap

We Cook the Best Food in the City

The Bear Trap is your go-to destination for delicious food, cold drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere. Our pizzas are fabulous and our burgers are fresh and fantastic!

We pride ourselves on serving excellent customer service, the best meals, and at the best prices. Whether you're stopping in for lunch, dinner, or our amazing nightly specials, we guarantee you'll leave satisfied.

Why Choose The Bear Trap?

  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients
  • Daily and weekly specials
  • Friendly, welcoming atmosphere
  • Great prices for great food
  • Full salad bar available on select nights
